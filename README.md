# Aarogya AI
India's first two-sided health intelligence platform 
built for low-income communities.

## Problem
500 million Indians lack access to affordable healthcare.
Average consultation fee of ₹300–800 acts as a barrier 
for daily wage earners. 58% of our survey respondents 
confirmed cost affects their doctor visit decisions.

## Solution
- Symptom triage — AI tells you if you need a doctor
- Doctor discovery — nearest specialists sorted by 
  distance, price and rating
- Community health — outbreak alerts for ASHA workers 
  and NGOs

## Model Performance
- Algorithm: Naive Bayes
- Accuracy: 99.3%
- Classification Error: 0.7%
- Models evaluated: 205
- Training data: 4,920 examples
- Diseases covered: 41
- Symptoms mapped: 132

## Tech Stack
- Altair RapidMiner AI Studio — predictive modeling
- Mendix Studio Pro — application interface

## Datasets
- Disease Symptom Prediction by Pranay Patil (Kaggle)
  — 61,134 downloads, widely validated
- Doctor Specialty Recommendation by Ebrahim Elgazar 
  (Kaggle)

## Primary Research
- Survey conducted across Nagpur — 19 respondents
- 58% say consultation fee affects doctor visit decisions
- 79% have Googled symptoms before visiting a doctor
- 74% would trust AI to recommend a nearby doctor
- Top factors when choosing a doctor: Rating, 
  Specialization, Proximity
